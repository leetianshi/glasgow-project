# APA 7th Edition
## 1. Rules about Citing
### 1.1 Work with a Single Author
Author prominent: <u> author surname| (year)| ... </u> <br>
> *Example*: Bryman (2012) recommends...

Information prominent: <u> ...| (author surname,| year) </u> <br>
> *Example*: ... (Bryman, 2012).

### 1.2 Work with Two Authors
Author prominent: <u> author surname and author surname| (year)| ... </u> <br>
> *Example*: Crisp and Turner (2014) note that being watched by others in a public event ...

Information prominent: <u> ...| (author surname & author surname,| year) </u> <br>
> *Example*: Nervousness can be caused by ... (Crisp & Turner, 2014).

### 1.3 Work with Three or More Authors
Author prominent: <u> first author surname| et al.| (year)| ... </u>
> *Example*: Crisp et al. (1996) note that making a weight biography could assist with the recovery ...

Information prominent: <u> ...| (first author surname| et al.,| year). </u>
> *Example*: ... a balanced diet will allow the nutritional balance to re-establish (Crisp et al., 1996).

### 1.4 No Date Can Be Found
use <u> n.d. </u> for "no date".
> *Example*: ... (Wilkinson, n.d.).
