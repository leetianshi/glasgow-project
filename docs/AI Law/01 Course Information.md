## Assessment
### Formative Assessment
**·** Up to **1,000 words** answer to a mock exam question </br> 
**·** Deadline: **13 February** </br> 
**·** Question will be released one week in advance (**06 February**) </br> 
**·** Feedback session: **27 February** </br> 
**Formative assessment is not mandatory** </br> 
It is an opportunity to attempt to answer an exam question in advance of the summative assessment </br> 
There are **no extensions!**

### Summative Assessment
**3-hour seen examination (100% final mark)** </br>
**·** You must answer 3 out of 6 questions </br>
**·** Max 3.750 words </br>
**·** Questions will be made available **at least** 48h in advance </br>
**·** Spring exam diet

**Everything will take place online!**
